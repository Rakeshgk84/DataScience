Project Overview
In this assignment, I will use machine learning techniques to perform data analysis and learn models about our data. I will use a real world music dataset from Last.fm for this assignment. There are two parts to this assignment: In the first part I will look at Unsupervised Learning with clustering and in the second part, I will study Supervised Learning. The play data (and user/artist matrix) comes from the Last.fm 1K Users dataset, while the tags come from the Last.fm Music Tags dataset. I didnt have to interact with these datasets directly to cleaned it up or filter what what i want, because it was already preprocessed for use.
